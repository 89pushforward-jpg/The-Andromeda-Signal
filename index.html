The premise

What is The Andromeda Signal?

The Andromeda Signal is an interactive sci-fi game where your choices shape the story. You intercept a transmission from the Andromeda galaxy — and through Lyra, a girl from the distant world of Auralis, you uncover the mystery of the Signal: a force slowly awakening across the universe.

The series
